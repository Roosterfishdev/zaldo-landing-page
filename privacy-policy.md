# Privacy Policy — Zaldo

**Last updated: June 25, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Jaguar Hosting LLC ("Jaguar Hosting," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use Zaldo (the "Service"), including the Zaldo web dashboard, the Zaldo Google Workspace (Gmail) add-on, and any related applications or services.

Zaldo is a personal expense-tracking service that helps you understand your spending by reading the transaction notification emails your bank already sends you and organizing them into a clear financial dashboard.

## 2. Information we collect

### 2.1 Information you provide
- **Account information:** your email address (used to create and access your account via Google sign-in or magic link).
- **Manually entered data:** transactions, categories, spending limits, rules, currency preferences, and other information you choose to enter into Zaldo.
- **Payment information:** when you subscribe to a paid plan, your payment details are collected and processed directly by Stripe. We do **not** receive or store your full card number or payment credentials. We receive only limited billing information (such as subscription status, billing interval, and a Stripe customer identifier).

### 2.2 Information from Google (Gmail add-on)
If you choose to install and authorize the Zaldo Gmail add-on, Zaldo accesses **only** the specific bank notification email that is currently open, in order to detect and extract transaction details (such as the merchant name, amount, date, card identifier, and transaction type).

- Zaldo reads the content of a bank notification email **only when you open it** and the add-on is active.
- Zaldo extracts **only** the transaction data needed to create an expense record.
- Zaldo does **not** read, scan, index, store, or back up your other emails.
- Zaldo does **not** access your Google account password and does **not** access your online banking.

### 2.3 Information collected automatically
- **Usage and analytics data:** we use privacy-focused analytics to understand how the Service is used (for example, pages visited and features used). 
- **Technical data:** basic technical information such as browser type and approximate region, used to operate and secure the Service.

## 3. Google API Services — Limited Use Disclosure

Zaldo's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the [Google API Services User Data Policy](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy), including the **Limited Use** requirements.

Specifically:

- **Limited purpose:** Information obtained from Gmail through Google Workspace APIs is used **solely** to provide and improve Zaldo's expense-tracking features that you have requested — namely, detecting and recording a transaction from a bank notification email you open.
- **No permanent copies of email:** Zaldo does **not** create permanent copies of your email messages, does **not** build databases of your email, and does **not** retain cached copies of your email beyond what is necessary to perform the requested action.
- **No human access:** We do **not** allow humans to read your Gmail data, except (a) with your explicit consent for specific messages, (b) where necessary for security purposes (such as investigating abuse), (c) to comply with applicable law, or (d) where the data has been aggregated and anonymized. 
- **No advertising:** We do **not** use Gmail data for advertising purposes.
- **No model training:** We do **not** use your Gmail data, or any Google user data, to train, develop, or improve generalized or non-personalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models.
- **No sale or transfer:** We do **not** sell Google user data and do **not** transfer it to third parties except as necessary to provide the Service, for security purposes, or to comply with applicable law.

What Zaldo retains is the **resulting transaction record** that you choose to add (merchant, amount, date, card identifier, type), stored in your Zaldo account — not the underlying email.

## 5. How we share your information

We do **not** sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers that help us operate Zaldo, and only as necessary:

- **Google** — for authentication and the Gmail add-on functionality, subject to Section 3.
- **Supabase** — our database and authentication infrastructure provider, where your account data and transaction records are stored.
- **Stripe** — our payment processor, which handles subscriptions and payment data. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).
- **Resend** — our email delivery provider, used to send transactional emails such as login links.
- **Hosting and analytics providers** — used to host the Service and understand usage in a privacy-focused manner.

We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Jaguar Hosting, our users, or others.

## 8. Revoking access to Gmail

You may revoke Zaldo's access to your Gmail at any time through your Google Account settings, at [https://myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions). Once revoked, Zaldo will no longer be able to access any Gmail data. Transaction records you previously added to Zaldo will remain in your account unless you delete them or your account.
